A Smarter Way to Reduce Food Waste and Fight Hunger

Our Mission

At FoodConnect, we connect surplus food from restaurants, event venues, and suppliers to vulnerable communities in need. By optimizing food distribution and minimizing waste, we contribute to a sustainable future while addressing food insecurity.

How It Works

1. Collect

Restaurants, event venues, and suppliers list surplus food on the platform, ensuring it is fresh and safe for consumption.

2. Connect

Our system matches surplus food with registered charities, shelters, and community organizations in real time.

3. Deliver

Optimized logistics ensure swift and efficient delivery of food to those who need it most.

Features

Real-Time Listings

Smart Matching Algorithm
