About Us

FoodShare is a revolutionary platform that connects restaurants, event venues, and food suppliers with organizations serving vulnerable populations. Our mission is to reduce food waste while ensuring that no one goes hungry.

Our Features

We've built powerful tools to make food donation simple, efficient, and impactful.

Real-time Donations

Connect instantly with food donors and recipients in your area to facilitate quick and efficient food transfers.

Smart Distribution

Our intelligent system optimizes delivery routes to ensure food reaches those in need quickly and efficiently.

Impact Tracking

Monitor your contribution to reducing food waste and feeding communities with detailed analytics and reports.
